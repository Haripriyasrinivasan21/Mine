About Child care

Our Services is concerned with the physical, social and psychological well-being of children, particularly children suffering from the effects of poverty or lacking normal parential care and supervision. Services may apply such as Feeding, Shelter and simple health precautions. Need For ChildWelfare: 1 million out of 20 million babies born every yer in India are abandoned soon after their birth due to different spcio-economic reasons. Child Labour, Mall Nutrition and No Education for the children are the major problems in our country to overcome this we need a organzation that helps poor children and provide thier basic needs and facilities. The Food, Clothes you through out, are dream for many children. Helping Hands are Better than Praying Lips.
    Our Features
  • Family like care.
  • Advocacy and child safeguarding.
  • Education.
  • Children’s Participation.
  • Youth Engagement as Volunteers
  • Kinship care
